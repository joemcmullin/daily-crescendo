The short version

This is a pre-launch website. The only information we collect is what you choose to type into the reservation form — your email and a few answers — so we can email you when the app launches. We don't run ad trackers or analytics here, and we never sell your data. The app itself is being built to keep your practice data on your device, with no account and nothing sent to us.

1. Who we are

Practice (a working name) is being built by Apex Development Studio LLC ("we," "us"). If you have any question about this policy or your data, contact us at REPLACE_WITH_CONTACT.

2. This website (pre-launch)

This site exists to describe the app and let interested people reserve a founding price. It is informational and there is no account, login, or payment.

What we collect

Only what you submit in the reservation form:

  • Your email address.
  • Your answers to a few short questions (how you currently track practice, how often you practice, what would make you switch, and whether you're a music teacher).

We do not use cookies, advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, fingerprinting, or session recording on this page. If you never submit the form, we receive nothing about you.

How the form works

The reservation form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service that receives your submission and forwards it to us by email. Your information is handled under Formspree's own privacy terms in addition to this policy.

Why we collect it

  • To email you once, when the app launches (and, at most, brief related updates you can opt out of at any time).
  • To understand demand and build a better product.

Legal bases (where applicable)

Where the GDPR or similar laws apply, we rely on your consent (which you give by submitting the form) and our legitimate interest in launching and improving the app.

Retention

We keep reservation data only as long as needed to notify you at launch and improve the product, and we delete it sooner on request.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your information, or to stop contacting you, at any time — just email REPLACE_WITH_CONTACT. We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. The app (when it launches)

The Practice app is being designed to be private by architecture, not just by promise:

  • No account. You won't create a login to use it.
  • On-device. Your sessions, notes, streaks, and recordings are stored on your iPhone and your own private iCloud — not on our servers.
  • On-device AI. Session recaps are generated on your device using Apple's on-device intelligence. Your practice content is not uploaded to us or any third party to do this.
  • No analytics SDKs. Our goal at launch is an App Store "Data Not Collected" privacy label.

This section describes our current design intent for the unreleased app and will be finalized in the app's own privacy label and policy at launch.

4. Children

This website is intended for adults reserving early access and is not directed to children under 13. Because the app is designed to store data on the device with no collection by us, it does not gather personal information from children even when a younger student uses it. We will confirm the app's children's-privacy posture in its App Store listing at launch.

5. International users

We operate from the United States. If you contact us from outside the U.S., you understand your information may be processed in the U.S. Residents of the EEA/UK and of U.S. states with privacy laws (such as California) have rights to access and delete their data, which we honor — email us to exercise them.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product develops. We'll change the "last updated" date above, and for material changes affecting people who've already reserved, we'll note it when we email you.
